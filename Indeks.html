London

The city of London is also known as one of the cities in the world that pays close attention to its old building area. If you have a vacation to London, you can definitely see firsthand how the citizens and government of London really pay attention to every detail of old buildings built from tens to hundreds of years ago. Starting from train stations, office areas, to shopping centers, everything is neatly arranged. For those of you who really like the old city area, then traveling to London is definitely a paradise for you.
